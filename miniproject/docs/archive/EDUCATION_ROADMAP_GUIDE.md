# Education Roadmap System - Quick Reference Guide

## What Changed?

The career roadmap system now creates **specific, data-driven paths** from your **current education to next step** - extracted from your uploaded PDF. No more random suggestions!

## How It Works

### Step 1: Complete Your Profile
- Go to [Profile](/profile)
- Update **Current Education Level** (e.g., "12th Grade", "B.Tech", "B.Sc")
- Add your interests, skills, and academic score
- ✓ This is REQUIRED to generate a roadmap

### Step 2: Upload Your Career Prediction Report
- Go to [Career Roadmap](/career-roadmap)
- Click **Upload File** button
- Upload your career prediction PDF
- System extracts your **next education goal** automatically
- ✓ This is REQUIRED to generate a roadmap

### Step 3: Generate Your Roadmap
- Click **CREATE YOUR EDUCATION ROADMAP** button
- System processes:
  - ✓ Current education from profile
  - ✓ Next step from PDF
  - ✓ Your interests & skills
  - ✓ Education progression database
- Generates comprehensive roadmap

### Step 4: View Your Structured Roadmap
Roadmap shows:
- **Timeline**: What to do in months 1-2, 3-6, 7-12, after 12
- **Phases**: 5-7 sequential phases with concrete tasks
- **Projects**: 3+ practical projects per transition
- **Resources**: Links to courses, portals, materials
- **Common Mistakes**: Pitfalls to avoid
- **Success Criteria**: How to know you're on track
- **Cost Estimate**: Approximate expenses involved

## Common Questions

**Q: My profile education and PDF goal don't match available pathways?**
- A: System shows closest pathway and explains. Update profile if education level changed.

**Q: Can I download the roadmap?**
- A: Yes! Click "Download Roadmap" to get a text file version.

**Q: Can I save this to my dashboard?**
- A: Yes! Click "Save to Dashboard" to access later from your profile.

**Q: What if I want to change my next education goal?**
- A: Upload a different PDF with new goal, then regenerate.

**Q: Are the resources links free?**
- A: Mix of free and paid. Each resource shows its type (Free/Paid/Freemium).

**Q: How long does each roadmap take?**
- A: Depends on pathway. 2 years for degree, 6-12 months for job transition, etc.

**Q: Can I modify the phases after generation?**
- A: The generated roadmap is a template. You can adjust based on your pace.

## Pro Tips

1. **Set Reminders**: Use the timeline to set monthly milestones
2. **Track Progress**: Mark completed phases in your dashboard
3. **Update Skills**: As you complete phases, update your profile with new skills
4. **Find Mentors**: Many resources include community sections - join for guidance
5. **Document Projects**: Keep portfolio of projects you complete from the roadmap
6. **Adapt Timeline**: If you're ahead/behind, adjust subsequent phases accordingly
7. **Share Roadmap**: Download and share with mentors for feedback
